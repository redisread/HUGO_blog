### 使用自定义文字样式

输入代码:

```html
<span style="font-size:2rem; background:yellow;">**Bigger**</span>
```

<span style="font-size:2rem; background:yellow;">**Bigger**</span>

### 设置键盘按键

输入代码:

```html
<kbd>Ctrl</kbd>+<kbd>F9</kbd>
```
