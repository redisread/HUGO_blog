## 推文 2: AI Agent将杀死App Store

**作者:** @aakashgupta
**发布时间:** Tue May 05 05:02:15 +0000 2026
**互动数据:** 39 likes, 7 replies, 5 retweets

**原文:**
Elon told Joe Rogan every app on your phone will be dead in 5 years.

$43 billion in App Store and Google Play commissions is what dies first.

Apple's App Store moved $1.3 trillion in billings last year. The slice that faces commission is $131 billion in digital goods and services. Apple's take on that runs $30 billion globally per analyst estimates. Google Play adds another $13 billion. Combined, the app store layer collects $43 billion a year in rent on software distribution.

The agent flow runs like this. The user types "book me a flight to LA" into Grok, ChatGPT, or Claude. The agent calls United, Expedia, and Kayak APIs. It compares prices. It books the cheapest. Expedia still exists. Kayak still exists. United's reservation system still exists. What disappears is the user opening three apps and copying details into a fourth.

The agent moved up a layer.

This prediction has a track record. In 2012 Mark Zuckerberg called HTML5 Facebook's biggest mistake after betting against native apps. The native apps won. In 2014 Facebook M and the Slack bot wave were going to replace apps. They didn't. In 2017 Alexa was going to make app interfaces obsolete. The Echo sold and apps kept growing. Every interface shift gets predicted to kill apps. The apps survive underneath.

The reason is what apps actually own. X owns the social graph and the identity layer. Spotify owns the licensing deals and the catalog. DoorDash owns the merchant network. Uber owns the driver supply. Stripe owns the payment rails. None of that lives in the UI. The UI is the cheapest part of the stack to replace.

What changes is who collects rent. Apple collects rent today because it controls discovery and payments. If the AI agent becomes how users find and pay for software, that $43 billion commission pool becomes contestable for the first time since 2008. The agent picks which app fulfills the request. The agent picks whether to take a cut.

Elon is building the agent. Grok with X integration, Tesla integration, Starlink integration, Optimus integration. OpenAI is racing for the same layer. So is Anthropic. Three labs are competing to be what sits above the App Store.

The prediction is the product.

**翻译:**
埃隆告诉乔·罗根，你手机上的每个应用将在5年内消亡。

首先消亡的是App Store和Google Play每年430亿美元的佣金收入。

苹果App Store去年处理了1.3万亿美元的交易额。其中面临抽成的数字商品和服务部分为1310亿美元。据分析师估计，苹果从中抽取约300亿美元。Google Play再增加130亿美元。合计起来，应用商店层每年从软件分发中收取430亿美元的"租金"。

AI代理的工作流程是这样的：用户在Grok、ChatGPT或Claude中输入"帮我订一张去洛杉矶的机票"。代理调用美联航、Expedia和Kayak的API，比较价格，预订最便宜的。Expedia仍然存在，Kayak仍然存在，美联航的预订系统仍然存在。消失的是用户打开三个应用并把详情复制到第四个应用的过程。

代理向上移动了一层。

这个预测有迹可循。2012年马克·扎克伯格在押注HTML5对抗原生应用后，称HTML5是Facebook最大的错误。原生应用赢了。2014年Facebook M和Slack机器人浪潮被认为将取代应用。它们没有。2017年Alexa被认为将使应用界面过时。Echo卖出了，应用继续增长。每次界面变革都被预测会杀死应用。应用在底层存活下来。

原因是应用真正拥有的是什么。X拥有社交图谱和身份层。Spotify拥有授权协议和曲库。DoorDash拥有商户网络。Uber拥有司机供应。Stripe拥有支付通道。这些都不在UI中。UI是技术栈中最容易被替换的部分。

改变的是谁收取租金。苹果今天收取租金是因为它控制发现和支付。如果AI代理成为用户寻找和支付软件的方式，那么自2008年以来首次，这430亿美元的佣金池变得可争夺。代理选择哪个应用来满足请求。代理决定是否收取分成。

埃隆正在构建代理。Grok与X集成、特斯拉集成、星链集成、Optimus集成。OpenAI也在竞逐同一层。Anthropic也是如此。三个实验室正在竞争成为位于App Store之上的存在。

这个预测就是产品本身。

**核心观点:**
AI Agent不会杀死应用本身（因为应用拥有数据、网络和供应链等核心资产），但会杀死App Store的430亿美元佣金模式。代理将成为新的发现层，取代应用商店的位置，改变"谁收取租金"的权力结构。

**可实践建议:**
- 关注AI Agent作为新入口层的投资机会
- 思考你的业务如何与Agent集成而非仅依赖App Store
- 重视API设计，因为Agent将通过API而非UI访问服务
- 考虑多模型策略，不同Agent平台可能有不同的集成方式

- 探讨AI Agent时代的商业模式创新
- 预测5年后手机主屏幕会是什么样子
